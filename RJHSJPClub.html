Basics of Japanese Culture

Throughout its history, Japan has formed a unique culture full of both modern and ancient traditions. Japan and Japanese people in general are notorious for having a strong sense of aestetics which can definitely be seen in many things that constitute Japanese culture.

Basics of Japanese Language

As one of the languages that shares the amount of differences with English, Japanese has many interesting and surprising qualities. However, no one should be discouraged from studying it and it is very worth while to learn.

Basics of Japanese History

Japanese history goes back many thousands of years and is remarkable due to the many cultural and social that have occured within Japan's time as an historical entity.

Basics of Japanese Food

Japanese food known in Japanese as nihonshyoku includes wide range of various dishes created through centeries of culinary innovation and is influenced by many different cultures from around the world.
